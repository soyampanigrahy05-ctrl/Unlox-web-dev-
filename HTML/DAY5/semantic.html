SEMANTIC

Semantic HTML is the use of HTML markup to convey meaning and structure, rather than just presentation.

Benefits of Semantic HTML

  • Improves accessibility for screen readers and assistive technologies.
  • Enhances SEO by providing meaningful content structure.
  • Facilitates easier maintenance and readability of code.

Common Semantic Elements

  • <header>: Represents introductory content or a set of navigational links.
  • <nav>: Represents a section of navigation links.
  • <main>: Represents the main content of the document.
  • <article>: Represents a self-contained piece of content that could be distributed independently.
  • <section>: Represents a thematic grouping of content, typically with a heading.
  • <footer>: Represents the footer for its nearest sectioning content or sectioning root element.
